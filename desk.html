Chairman Desk

Towards the end of seventees, three souls, later joined by four more, stirred by their faith, social consciousness and patriotic ferver, initiated a journey to educate vulnerables of the society. This initiative gave birth to the Deepalaya Schools for educating the poor, needy, deserving, marginalized and denied. It took momentum with 5 students on day one, 16th July 1979 - and no looking back. The efforts of three decades returned handsome dividends and over 50000 children educated. The outcomes have been satisfying and is appreciated. Deepalaya School, Kalkaji Extn. is a recognized and affiliated school. To reach this level, one had to undertake quite a bit of struggles. However, the efforts were worthwhile, for the management could uphold and live its value's and could comply with all stipulations and conditions. Hence Deepalaya Schools today standout prominently as an institution fulfilling the mission of social obligation, professional standards and undiluted ethics. Let God almighty enable us to hold on and grow, to reach heights to serve larger numbers, who have “Rights” as we understand our obligations. Secretary & CEO others and I see a good future through the thoughts shared by them.

~ SHRI. T.M. ABRAHAM

Manager's Desk

From the humble beginning in 1979, from a rented building with five children, Deepalaya School has grown both in size and stature. Now we have six schools in three states with more than four thousand students on the role. I am happy that I am associated with Deepalaya from the beginning – as a treasure for a long time and at present as the manager of the schools. It is because of the sincere effort and commitment of the people attached to Deepalaya the present position has evolved. We have a rich heritage, tradition, culture and values which have to be maintained and reflected through education. That is what Deepalaya is striving to do. In the past we tried to keep certain values in our work which we will continue to keep for the improvement of the quality of life. I am confident that Deepalaya Parivar has the ability and commitment to work with and for others and I see a good future through the thoughts shared by them.

~ DR. GEORGE JOHN

Principal's Desk

Deepalaya - Kalkaji School(DSKE) is one of the enabling vehicles is realising Deepalaya’s nobel aspirations of helping children from the weaker sections of society. This beautiful journey was initiated 36 years ago by Mr. T. K. Mathew, Mr. Y. Chakochan, Mr. T. M. Abraham, Mr. C. M. Mathai, Mr. Punnose Thomas, Mr. P. J. Thomas and Mrs. Grace Thomas. With their guidance and support we have grown from a small seed to a blossomed tree. The school has now received recognition from the Directorate of education and affiliation from the CBSE board is testament to our unwavering commitment to providing quality education and equal opportunities to our students. The curriculum and pedagogy is designed to not only provide our students with quality education but also to equip them with life sustaining skills. We believe that each child is uniquely gifted and seek to provide them with a plethora of opportunities to leverage their potential. Thus art, music, dance, sports and many such activities from an integral part of their education. Students are encouraged to participate in various co-curricular activities organised by Interact Club, Health Club, Eco Club, Computer Club, and Inter House Competitions. It is indeed, very rewarding to see a gleam in the eyes and a confident smile on the faces of my students as they give shape each day to their dreams inspite of their hardships.

~ Shaju PP
